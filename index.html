About Us

Founded in 2005, Agrawal Jain & Co. (AJC) is a leading Chartered Accountants firm in Chhattisgarh providing Audit, Management Consultancy, Direct & Indirect Tax Consultancy, Accounting Services, Corporate & Legal Compliance, Business Setup, Project Financing, ROC & Tax Return Filing, and many more solutions. With over 18+ years of expertise and a team of highly competent professionals, we deliver efficacious solutions tailored to client needs.

Our philosophy is to partner with clients, banishing the ‘one-size-fits-all’ standardization. Under the leadership of our Founder Partner CA Rajesh Agrawal, and with a strong team of 50+ professionals, AJC continues to build trust, provide seamless value, and enjoy an unparalleled reputation.
